# Case Study – “Citrus Boost” Mini Launch

## Context
Bloomy Drinks wanted to promote their new flavor “Citrus Boost” over a short two-week period.  
The goal was to introduce the flavor, educate the audience, and generate initial engagement.

---

## My Role
• Content planning  
• Designing 3 key visuals in Canva  
• Writing all short-form copy  
• Organizing a micro content calendar for the launch  
• Documenting the internal creative direction

---

## What I Did
• Created a content calendar with 8 pieces for Instagram and TikTok  
• Designed 3 main visuals featuring the new flavor  
• Wrote platform-specific copy  
• Structured the mini campaign into awareness → engagement → conversion  
• Created a brief to align message, tone and audience

---

## Outputs
• 3 Canva visuals  
• 8 content posts  
• 1 simple funnel for the launch  
• 1 set of copy variations  

---

## Learnings
• UGC (even simulated) consistently outperformed branded visuals in engagement  
• Vibrant color palettes worked better than neutral tones for product launches  
• Short, direct copy had stronger retention on social platforms  
• Consistency in color and typography improved brand recall
